About NGOMA RentHub

NGOMA RentHub was created to connect people with affordable, local housing options—especially students and community members in need of short or long-term rentals in NGOMA district. Our platform is simple, fast, and easy to use for both tenants and landlords.

Our Mission

We aim to make housing accessible by building a platform that focuses on trust, transparency, and local availability. Whether you're a student looking for a room or a landlord with a space to offer, NGOMA RentHub is here to help you connect.
